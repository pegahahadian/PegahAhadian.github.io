About Me

I am a Graduate Research Assistant at Kent State University, pursuing a Ph.D. in Computer Science with a keen focus on using artificial intelligence and its trustworthiness to innovate in health science and education. My previous roles as a software developer enabled me to design intuitive and secure online experiences, With a strong academic background, including a Master's in Computer Science from Shahid Beheshti University and numerous publications, I am deeply committed to advancing my knowledge and contributing positively to the broader community. I am also honored to have received a full scholarship for my doctoral studies, which fuels my passion for research and development in AI.
